What are clinical studies?

Clinical studies includes research by utilizing human volunteers, or participants, to further medicinal learning. The two primary forms of clinical studies are interventional studies and observational studies

What are the benefits?

Clinical studies are important to the further development of medical research and treatments for different types of diseases, illness's and procedures. As well as facilitating new ways to detect, diagnose, and reduce the risk of disease.

What is the impact?

Clinical studies have helped medical researchers gain a further understanding of treatments and procedures to help diagnose patients more accurately and make medical discoveries that have gone past the scope of the studies
